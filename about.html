About Voyager Beach Resort

Nyali Beach, Mombasa - Managed by Heritage Hotels

Overview

Voyager Beach Resort is a cruise-themed beachfront getaway located along the white sands of Nyali Beach in Mombasa. Known for its vibrant atmosphere, family-friendly facilities, and all-inclusive service, the resort mimics a ship setting and offers themed evenings, entertainment, and water sports.

Accommodation

The resort offers 236 cabins, including garden view, sea view, and executive options. Each room includes:

  • Air conditioning
  • Satellite TV
  • Mini-fridge
  • Private balcony
  • In-room safe

Dining & Bars

Voyager provides multiple themed restaurants and bars:

  • Mashua Restaurant – buffet dining
  • Smugglers’ Cove – seafood grill
  • Minestrone Restaurant – Italian cuisine
  • Captain’s Table – outdoor terrace dining

Facilities & Activities

  • Three swimming pools (including an adults-only infinity pool)
  • Water sports: snorkeling, windsurfing, boat rides
  • Fitness center, tennis courts, beach volleyball
  • Kids’ club & nightly entertainment

Sustainability

Voyager is Eco Silver Rated by Ecotourism Kenya and actively supports local conservation efforts near Mombasa Marine Park.
